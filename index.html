About Me

Kevin Nguyen, with a rich backdrop of computer networking and military electronics, anchors himself as a leading Aviation Engineering Specialist in Redmond, Washington, pushing the frontiers of space satellite innovations. A 15-year seasoned professional, Kevin's voyage began in the U.S. Navy, equipping him with an indomitable foundation in electronics. His commitment to excellence reflects in his robust skills ranging from electronics assembly and soldering to hardware testing. As an IPC 610/620/J-standard and 7711/7722 certified specialist, his fingerprints are distinct on groundbreaking projects like the Starlink HITL v1.0, Starlink v1.5, and both Starlink v2.0 full size and 2.0 mini. Connect with Kevin on LinkedIn to follow his transformative journey, as he steers his expertise from hardware realms into the dynamic software industry, eyeing a future in QA testing. Away from the circuits and satellites, Kevin is driven by a fervor for personal and professional evolution, always aiming for the next orbit in his career.
